## Industry Benchmarks

### SaaS Industry
- Average sales cycle: 45-90 days (SMB), 6-12 months (Enterprise)
- Average deal size: $15K-$50K (SMB), $100K-$500K (Enterprise)
- Win rate benchmark: 20-30% from qualification to close
- Key decision-makers: VP of Engineering, CTO, Director of IT

### Healthcare Technology
- Average sales cycle: 6-18 months (compliance requirements lengthen cycle)
- Average deal size: $200K-$2M (due to compliance & integration scope)
- Win rate benchmark: 15-25% (high compliance bar filters deals)
- Key decision-makers: VP of Product, CISO, CMO, CTO
- Must-have: HIPAA/SOC2 compliance, data residency options

### Retail / E-commerce
- Average sales cycle: 3-9 months
- Average deal size: $50K-$300K
- Win rate benchmark: 20-35%
- Key decision-makers: VP of Operations, Director of IT, CTO
- Peak evaluation periods: Post-holiday (Jan-Mar), Pre-holiday prep (Jun-Aug)

### Professional Services / Consulting
- Average sales cycle: 30-60 days (faster decision cycles)
- Average deal size: $25K-$150K
- Win rate benchmark: 25-40%
- Key decision-makers: Managing Partner, Director of Operations
- Key drivers: Consultant utilization, knowledge management, client reporting
