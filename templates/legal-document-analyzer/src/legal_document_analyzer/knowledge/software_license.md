# Sample Software License Agreement

**Agreement Title**: Enterprise Software License Agreement
**Document ID**: SLA-2026-001
**Effective Date**: February 1, 2026
**Parties**: CloudTech Solutions ("Licensor") and Global Retail Corp. ("Licensee")

## 1. Definitions

**"Software"** means the CloudTech Enterprise Platform version 5.0, including all modules, components, documentation, and updates provided under this Agreement.

**"Authorized Users"** means employees and contractors of Licensee who are authorized to access and use the Software, up to the number specified in the Order Form.

**"Order Form"** means the document referencing this Agreement that specifies the Software, number of Authorized Users, fees, and subscription term.

## 2. License Grant

Subject to the terms of this Agreement and payment of all applicable fees, Licensor grants Licensee a **non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable** license to:
(a) Install and use the Software for Licensee's internal business operations;
(b) Allow up to **500 Authorized Users** to access the Software;
(c) Create reasonable backup copies for disaster recovery purposes.

## 3. License Restrictions

Licensee shall NOT:
(a) Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Software;
(b) Modify, adapt, or create derivative works based on the Software;
(c) Sublicense, lease, rent, or lend the Software to any third party;
(d) Use the Software to provide services to third parties (no SaaS reselling);
(e) Remove or alter any proprietary notices, labels, or marks;
(f) Exceed the number of Authorized Users without purchasing additional licenses.

## 4. Fees and Payment

**Annual License Fee**: $120,000 USD per year
**Payment Terms**: Net 30 from invoice date
**Late Payment**: Interest at 1.5% per month on overdue amounts
**Fee Increases**: Licensor may increase fees by up to **5% annually** upon 90 days written notice before each renewal term.

## 5. Term and Termination

**Initial Term**: Three (3) years from the Effective Date.
**Renewal**: Automatically renews for successive **one (1) year** periods unless either party provides **ninety (90) days** written notice of non-renewal.

**Termination for Cause**: Either party may terminate if the other party:
(a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within **thirty (30) days** of written notice;
(b) Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases operations.

**Effect of Termination**: Upon termination:
(a) All licenses immediately terminate;
(b) Licensee shall cease all use of the Software within **fifteen (15) days**;
(c) Licensee shall destroy all copies and certify destruction in writing;
(d) No refund of prepaid fees unless termination is due to Licensor's breach.

## 6. Intellectual Property

All right, title, and interest in the Software, including all intellectual property rights, remain exclusively with Licensor. Licensee acquires no ownership rights through this Agreement.

Any feedback, suggestions, or improvements provided by Licensee to Licensor regarding the Software shall become the property of Licensor without obligation or compensation.

## 7. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information using the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care. The confidentiality obligations shall survive for **three (3) years** after termination.

**Licensor Warranties**:
(a) The Software will perform materially in accordance with the documentation for **twelve (12) months** from delivery;
(b) Licensor has the right to grant the license under this Agreement;

**Warranty Disclaimer**: EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED ABOVE, THE SOFTWARE IS PROVIDED "AS IS." LICENSOR DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT.

## 9. Limitation of Liability

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, SPECIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOSS OF PROFITS, DATA, OR BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES.

**Liability Cap**: Each party's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the **fees paid by Licensee in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim**.

**Exceptions**: The liability cap does NOT apply to:
(a) Breaches of confidentiality obligations;
(b) Intellectual property infringement;
(c) Gross negligence or willful misconduct.

## 10. Indemnification

**Licensor Indemnification**: Licensor shall indemnify Licensee against claims that the Software infringes a third party's intellectual property rights, provided that Licensor shall have no obligation if the infringement arises from:
(a) Modification of the Software by Licensee;
(b) Use of the Software in combination with non-Licensor products;
(c) Use of a non-current version when a non-infringing version is available.

**Licensee Indemnification**: Licensee shall indemnify Licensor against claims arising from Licensee's use of the Software in violation of this Agreement or applicable law.

## 11. Support and Maintenance

Licensor shall provide:
(a) **Standard Support**: Business hours (9 AM - 6 PM ET, Mon-Fri), email and phone support, 8-hour response time for critical issues;
(b) **Updates**: Bug fixes and minor updates included in the license fee;
(c) **Major Upgrades**: Available at additional cost as specified in the Order Form;
(d) **SLA**: 99.5% uptime for cloud-hosted components, measured monthly.

## 12. Data Protection

Licensor shall:
(a) Process Licensee data only as necessary to provide the Software;
(b) Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures;
(c) Notify Licensee of any data breach within **72 hours** of discovery;
(d) Comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR where applicable.

## 13. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the **State of California**, without regard to conflict of laws principles. Any disputes shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in **San Francisco County, California**.

## 14. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, government action, pandemic, or infrastructure failures, provided the affected party gives prompt notice and uses reasonable efforts to mitigate.

This Agreement, together with all Order Forms, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. No modification shall be effective unless in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both parties.
