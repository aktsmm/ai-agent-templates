# Product FAQ - Sample Knowledge Base

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?
Visit https://example.com/signup and fill in your email and password.
You will receive a confirmation email within 5 minutes.

### What are the system requirements?
- Modern web browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+)
- Stable internet connection
- JavaScript enabled

## Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
Bank transfers are available for annual Enterprise plans.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription.
Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
No partial refunds are provided for monthly plans.

### Can I get a refund?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions.
Contact support@example.com within 14 days of your first payment.

## Technical Issues

### Why is the app running slowly?
Common causes:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Disable browser extensions
3. Check your internet connection speed
4. Try using a different browser

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email,
and follow the instructions in the reset email.

## Account

### How do I change my email address?
Go to Settings > Profile > Email. You will need to verify
your new email address before the change takes effect.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
All your data will be deleted within 30 days.

Yes, annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly billing.
